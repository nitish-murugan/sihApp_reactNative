1. Bottom Navigation Bar (NavBar):
 Home → Dashboard / Welcome screen
 Live → Real-time meter values
 Solar → Solar energy monitoring
 Wind → Wind energy monitoring
 Tickets → Support or fault tickets

2. Live Section (Meters)
 Voltage (V)
 Current (A)
 Power (W)
 Energy (kWh)
 Power Factor (unitless)
 Frequency (Hz)
 Harmonics (%)
Example:
 Meter 1
 Voltage: 230 V
 Current: 5.2 A
 Power: 1200 W
 Energy: 50 kWh
 Power Factor: 0.95
 Frequency: 50 Hz
 Harmonics: 3.2 %
(etc…. Meter 2, Meter 3, Meter 4.)

3. Solar
 Same Layout Like Meters
 Voltage: V
 Current: A
 Power: W
 Temperature: kWh

4. Wind
 Same Layout Like Meters
 Voltage: V
 Current: A
 Power: W
 Speed of the wind: kWh

5. Tickets Section
 Users can raise tickets for issues.
 Ticket ID
 Description
 Status (Open, In Progress, Resolved)
 Date &amp; Time
